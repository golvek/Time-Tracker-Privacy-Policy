Privacy Policy for SQR Time Tracker

Last updated: November 2025

The SQR Time Tracker Chrome extension does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users.

Information Collection

This extension stores all time-tracking data locally in your browser using Chrome's storage.local API. No information is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

Permissions

The permissions requested by this extension (such as storage and activeTab) are used solely to enable its core functionality. None of them are used to collect identifiable information about users.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
